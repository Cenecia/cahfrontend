DeNCAH is an online multiplayer adaptation of the popular party game Cards Against Humanity.

The game is played with a deck of cards containing prompts (Black Cards) and responses (White Cards).

The game is designed to be played with a group of friends, and the goal is to have fun and make each other laugh.

Warning! This game contains content that may be considered offensive or inappropriate for some players.

Creating a Game:

1 - Start by entering your name
2 - Click Start a new game
3 - Choose the settings for the game:
(more info on the settings below)
4 - Click Start it Up and the share the game link with your friends!
5 - Wait until all players have joined and click Start Game!

Joining a Game:

1 - Click the game link sent to you
2 - Enter your name
3 - Click Join Game

If you accidentally close your window, don't worry! Just re-open the page and it will ask if you want to rejoin the game.
However, do not have the game open in multiple tabs of the same browser!

Game Settings

Name of Game:
 - This name only appears when a player clicks your share link.
Sets:
 - Choose what decks to include in the game.
Turn Time Limit:
 - If the round is not over before this time limit, a winner will automatically be selected from the players who have submitted a card.
 - Any player who has not submitted a white card will be timed out, meaning the game will no longer wait for them to submit a white card and they will be skipped as the czar.
 - Submitting a white card will take a player out of time out.
Score Limit:
 - The number of points a player needs to win the game. Standard rules is 10.
Score Limit:
 - The number of points a player needs to win the game.
Player Limit:
 - The number of players who can join your game. Use this if you know exactly how many people will be played and don't want anyone else who might see the join link to play.
